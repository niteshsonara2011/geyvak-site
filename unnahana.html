The atelier flow

The first version can stay simple and human: listen, design, craft, deliver. No pressure to become a large shop before the language, trust, and first pieces are ready.

Custom pathways

Start with custom sari direction, tailored outfits, men’s heels styling, khadi wear, artistic home pieces, or performance looks. Each enquiry begins with the person, not a catalogue.

Small, careful, real

Unnahana can begin through expression-of-interest, one-to-one styling conversations, small custom experiments, pop-up display, and limited made-to-order pieces.
