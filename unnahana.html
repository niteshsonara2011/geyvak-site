Name meaning

Unbound, expressive, not stitched into limits.

The name Unnahana is used here as a Sanskrit-inspired expression of being unbound, unfettered, unrestricted, and rising beyond fixed limits.

For this project, it means fashion that holds imagination, performance, culture, queerness, dignity, comfort, and becoming — without forcing the body or spirit into one narrow form.

Public meaning: Unnahana means unbound expression. Wear what you imagine.

Phase 1 offer

What Unnahana can begin with

Performance looks

Costume concepts, styling direction, and expressive pieces for Bhavai, Retro Reva, stage, street performance, photoshoots, and cultural storytelling.

Custom pieces

Small-batch and pre-order clothing ideas, upcycled looks, corset bases, dupatta styling, ghaghara-inspired movement pieces, and art-led accessories.

Creative collaboration

Open to ethical collaboration with tailors, costume designers, textile artists, photographers, performers, event organisers, and production helpers.

Ethical working style

Slow fashion, clear consent, human dignity.

  • Respectful styling for all bodies, genders, cultures, and expressions.
  • No hidden pressure, no shame-based beauty language, and no exploitative collaboration.
  • Low-waste, reuse-first, repair-friendly, and nature-honoring where practical.
  • Clear communication before custom work, pricing, timelines, and delivery.
