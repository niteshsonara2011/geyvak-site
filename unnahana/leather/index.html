Responsible sourcing standard.

The public claim should stay honest: real leather, chosen carefully, made to last and repaired before replaced. Unnahana should prioritise reclaimed, deadstock, vintage, upcycled or verified by-product leather, and clearly state what is known and what still needs verification.

SourceRecord supplier, country, material origin and stock type.
ProofKeep invoices, batch details and tannery notes where available.
WasteUse offcuts for card holders, patches, handles and repair kits.
RepairOffer stitching, conditioning and hardware replacement.
LongevityDesign for daily use, not trend disposal.
DisclosureNever overclaim. Be clear, humble and specific.

Leather care

Care pages build trust and reduce waste.

Care content should appear on product pages, the website footer and packaging inserts.

Wipe

Use a soft dry cloth after wear.

Condition

Condition lightly every 3-6 months depending on use.

Store

Use wide hangers and avoid direct sun.

Protect

Protect from weather and avoid trapping damp leather.

Repair

Fix stitching, hardware and straps early.

Record

Keep product care and repair history.
