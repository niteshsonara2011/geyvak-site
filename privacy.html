Privacy Policy

Geyvak is built with care to serve nature. This page explains how personal information may be collected, used and respected when you visit Geyvak.com or contact Geyvak.

```

Last updated: 28 June 2026

What Geyvak may collect

Depending on the page or service you use, Geyvak may collect information you choose to provide, including:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your phone number where a form specifically asks for it
  • Your enquiry type, message, project details or booking preferences
  • Creative references, files, images or preferences you choose to share
  • Basic technical information collected by the website host or form provider

Why information is used

Information may be used to respond to enquiries, understand the service you are seeking, arrange an appointment, prepare a quote or scope, maintain relevant communication records, and improve the clarity and reliability of Geyvak services.

Please share only what is needed for your enquiry. Avoid sending unnecessary sensitive personal information through general website forms.

Storage and service providers

Geyvak may use selected service providers to host the website, receive form submissions, manage email or arrange bookings. These providers may process information only as needed to provide their service.

When a form or booking provider is connected, the relevant provider will be identified accurately in that pathway.

AI transparency

Geyvak may use AI tools to support research, drafting, workflow design or service delivery. AI is used as a support tool. Human review, judgement and responsibility remain central to final work.

Sharing information

Geyvak does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers needed to operate the requested service, comply with law, or protect safety and rights where reasonably necessary.

Access, correction and deletion

You may contact Geyvak to ask about access, correction or deletion of personal information held in connection with your enquiry, subject to reasonable legal and operational limits.

For privacy questions, contact hello@geyvak.com.

Adults-only services

Some services, including Retro Reva Call Line, are intended for adults aged 18 and over. Geyvak does not intentionally collect personal information from children through those services.

Changes to this policy

Geyvak may update this policy as services, forms or booking pathways develop. The newest version will always appear on this page.
