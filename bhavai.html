Folk theatre, feeling, movement, story.

Bhavaઈ Theatre Art is Geyvak's performance branch for Gujarati Bhavai-inspired storytelling, character work, costume transformation, public performance, workshops, cultural events, and production work.

Every public presentation should be planned with cultural dignity, rehearsal, and clear production responsibility.

Character rooms

Three character identities.

Sutradhar, Ranglo and Bhavaઈ Devi now have real visual slots with distinct colour accents.

Sutradhar

Earthy, grounded and dignified. The host-character who carries the audience into the world of Bhavaઈ.

Ochre · tan · warm brown

Ranglo

Public-facing, humorous, sharp and rhythmic. A character of satire, joy, social reflection and connection.

Patchwork · vibrant · mixed palette

Bhavaઈ Devi

Mystical, powerful and ceremonial. A presence carried through drape, ornament, rhythm and emotional force.

Burgundy · gold · midnight
