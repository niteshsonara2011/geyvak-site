Bhavai

Folk theatre, feeling, movement, story.

Bhavai Theatre Art is Geyvak's performance branch for Gujarati folk-theatre inspired storytelling, character work, costume transformation, public performance, workshops, cultural events, and production work.

This is a public direction page, not a final booking catalogue. Every public presentation should be planned with cultural dignity, safety, rehearsal, and clear production responsibility.
