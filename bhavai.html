Bhavaઈ

Folk theatre energy for movement, costume and story.

Bhavaઈ is Geyvak's performance-art branch inspired by Gujarat's Bhavai folk theatre tradition: open-space performance, expressive character work, music, humour, rhythm, social observation and public storytelling.

This is a contemporary artistic interpretation — not a historical replica. The website language stays theatrical, inclusive and production-focused.

Bhavai is known as a Gujarati folk-theatre form shaped by open performance, song, dialogue, humour, dance and character work. Geyvak's Bhavaઈ page translates that energy into modern stage, street and workshop contexts.
